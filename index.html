The Problem

Everyday purchasing and delivery in Ghana is still inconvenient, unreliable, and exclusionary.

People often need

  • Groceries, pharmacy items, food & household essentials
  • Urgent pickups
  • Fast help without owning a car or motorcycle
  • Affordable delivery for short distances and informal errands

Existing delivery models often assume

  • A dedicated logistics fleet
  • Merchants already integrated online
  • Drivers with vehicles
  • Formal addresses and high delivery fees

The gap

A large number of daily errands in Ghana are too small, too local, or too informal for traditional delivery platforms — yet they still matter deeply to customers.

"Today, many people in Ghana still rely on phone calls, favors, or ad hoc arrangements to get simple things bought and delivered. That creates friction, uncertainty, and missed economic opportunity."
The Solution

TorMaMe turns everyday errands into a trusted digital marketplace.

01

Post a request

A sender posts what they need bought and where it should be delivered.

02

Runner accepts

A nearby runner accepts the task.

03

Prepay & escrow

TorMaMe calculates pricing transparently. The sender prepays; funds held in escrow.

04

Deliver & release

Payment is released after delivery confirmation.

Product

A simple two-sided workflow designed for trust and speed.

For Senders
  • Create a request — item details, purchase location, delivery location, ETA, and item size
  • See a full price breakdown before confirming
  • Pay upfront
  • Track the request through fulfilment
For Runners
  • Discover nearby requests through push notifications or the requests page
  • Review request details: item price, commission, ETA, and locations
  • Fulfil and deliver
  • Receive reimbursement plus commission after confirmation
Trust & Safety
  • Account creation and identity workflows
  • Escrowed payments
  • One-time delivery confirmation code
  • Configurable notifications
  • Transaction history
Business Model

TorMaMe earns on every successful transaction.

Sender pays for the item
Runner commission is added
Platform fee is added TorMaMe keeps the platform fee
On confirmed delivery Runner receives item reimbursement + commission. TorMaMe retains the platform fee.
